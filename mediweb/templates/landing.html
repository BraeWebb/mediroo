Who are we?

Mediroo is a medication tracker and digital pillbox application designed to help patients remember when and how to take their medication.

Taking medication is not always a simple process, and in amongst the hustle and bustle of everyday life, remembering to take medication can often be a disruption. In fact, it has been found that around 50% of the time, Australians are not taking their prescriptions on schedule.

MediRoo aims to solve this problem by seamlessly integrating the process of remembering to take medication into the daily lives of its users.
